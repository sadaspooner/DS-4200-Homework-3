About Me

Hello! My name is Sada Spooner, and I have always had a deep love for food and cooking. Growing up, I spent countless hours in the kitchen with my dad, making dinner and experimenting with new recipes. These moments weren’t just about making dinner. It was an invaluable time where I could bond with family, embrace creativity, and discover the fun that comes from sharing food with loved ones. Cooking became second nature to me, and over time, it evolved from a simple tradition into one my greatest passions.

The first meal I vividly remember making was sushi. My dad had given it to me as a baby and I was hooked from the start. When we finally tried making it ourselves, it was a total disaster. Despite our failure, that experience stuck with me. Since then, I have explored a variety of cuisines, experimenting with flavors and ingredients from all over the world. I’ve even gotten good enough to make Thanksgiving and Christmas dinners.

This website showcases my journey with food. Some of my best memories are in the kitchen, whether it’s failing at a new recipe or nailing a dish everyone loves. Through food blogging I hope to share my experiences, recipes, and inspirations. Food has the power to bring people together and I hope my blog inspires you to explore new dishes and create lasting memories in the kitchen.
